Detroit Of South Asia

Chennai is the capital and largest city of Tamil Nadu.It has a very hot weather in summer and mild in winter.The Marina Beach situated here is the longest beach in India.It was earlier known as Madras during British period.English and Tamil are fundamental languages spoken here.

Trade And Economy

Due to its proximity to the sea,it has encouraged trading and enhancing its economy.It is also famous for shipbuilding.It is also famous for its automobile industry and is the largest manufacturer of automobile in South Asia.The Indian Headquarters of Honda and its manufacturing plants are located in Chennai. The motorbike manufacturing plants of Hero are also located here.
