Economic Capital Of India

Mumbai is known as the economic capital of India.It is located in Maharashtra in India.It contributes around 35% of India's GDP.It has a GDP of around $310B.It is the most densely populated city in India.The World One situated here is the tallest building in South Asia.

Trade And Econmy

Mumbai is economically and financially very prosper.Its proximity to the sea promotes economic and trading activities.Jawaharlal Nehru port situated here is the best seaport in India.It has major textile industries and software industries.Even the headquarters of many famous brands such as TCS,Aditya Birla,Reliance Group are also situated here.The BSE and NSE,one of the best stock exchanges in tne world, are situated here.
