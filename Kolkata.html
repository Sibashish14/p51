City Of Joy

Kolkata is located on the banks of river Hugli in West Bengal.It's GDP is about $200B.It was earlier known as Calcutta and was also the British capital of India.The first metro train in India ran here.It is the capital of West Bengal.The 42 situated here is the 2nd tallest skyscraper of India.

Trade And Economy

It was the most economically developed city in West Bengal but rapidly declined after Independence.Now, major textile,pharmaceutical and agriculture industries are situated here.These industries contribute to its economy.The software industry is also constantly developing in this city.This city has the largest port and economy in East India.
