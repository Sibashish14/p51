Hi-Tech City

Hyderabad is the capital and largest city of Telangana and Andhra Pradesh.It has a GDP of about $79B.It is a city that has been ruled by Tughlaqs,Bahmans,Cholas,Cheras,Kayakats,Tuluvas and Sangamas.It was the city that has been put into India's territory by force(war).

Trade And Economy

Its economy and development is based on Hi-Tech city.It is one of its satellite cities.It also relieves burden on the main city and creates oppurtunities for economic development.It's economy is also dependent on tourism.The Rajiv Gandhi International Airport is one of the largest airport of India.It has only one terminal.It has the headquarters of many giant companies.The Indian headquarters of Amazon is located in this city.It is the largest headquarters of Amazon company in the world.
